This case study documents how Marin Valet, a San Francisco Bay Area valet and transportation company, achieved nearly 500% revenue growth and grew its contractor team to 25-30 staff by consolidating financial operations on Bluevine Business Checking. The business uses Stripe payment processing, accounts payable tools, and virtual debit cards with spend limits to handle 15+ weekly contractor payments and complete payroll in under 30 minutes.
